M Style - Managing

High: Confident and decisive teamplayers with good managerial skills. Makes decisions instinctively. May be impatient and gets angry easily.

Low: Prone to indecision and insecurity. Has little to no desire to manage others or be a part of a group.

E Style - Enthusiastic

High: Cheerful and enthusiastic, they enjoy interacting with others and living in the moment. Practical, realistic, and opportunistic. May be reckless and impulsive.

Low: Prone to melancholy and rigidity. Vastly prefers alone time over the company of others.

T Style - Territorial

High: Willing to fight for independence and individuality. Authentic and not afraid of showing their real identity. May be overly eager to fight and seek conflict.

Low: Tends to be overly sensitive to conflict. Prefers fitting in rather than standing out.

A Style - Accurate

High: Often considered as gifted, they want to find the truth in everything. Highly analytical and empathetic. May be perfectionistic and overly serious.

Low: Prone to making careless mistakes and derpiness. Has trouble taking things seriously.
