The Adventurer

You are the type of person who is up for every new adventure. You enjoy a great deal of novelty in your life.

The Early Adopter

You always seek the next big thing. You are open to new experiences and you are the first to adopt new ideas.

The Nomad

You try out everything in order to find what aligns with your identity. Once you find it out, you stay loyal to it.

The Voyager

You drift thourgh life, and you enjoy the journey. You are generally relaxed and you essentially live in the moment.

The Time Traveler

You essentially travel in time, whether past or future. You enjoy examining the history of your life, and look at the future with a keen eye.

The Lifestyle Master

You try out different lifestyles, and you find out what to do with your life. You are the person who actually knows how to live.

The Maverick

You march to the beat of your own drum. You are not afraid of standing out and being different from others.

The Performer

You want to be the most popular person in the room. You enjoy performing in front of others and being the best.

The Specialist

Your interests are highly selective and personal to you. You are eager to share your deepest feelings and interests with others.

The Supporter

You are a true supporter of everyone, and you put others' needs above yours. You highly enjoy helping and serving others.

The Devotee

You are very loyal to the people you know as well as your interests. You are the person who knows everything about your friends.

The Enthusiast

You are highly enthusiastic and joyful. You know how to look at the bright side of life. You must be fun at parties, right?

The Deep Diver

You dive deep into whatever interests you. You want to know every detail of your current obsession.

The Collector

You are all about your favorites, and you have loads of them. You make peace with whatever you have, and you are genuinely satisfied with that.

The Comfort Seeker

You want to be as comfortable as you want to be in life. You are very reliable and you have high standards for your experiences.

The Connoiseur

You take life seriously and you quietly support your community. You focus on one thing in order to master it.
