Pioneer

Pioneers are adaptable, imaginative, and risk takers. They make quick decisions and are emotionally expressive. This style is also trusting when it comes to people and they shrug off mistakes easily. They struggle the most with overly structured and unchanging enviroments.

Driver

Drivers are competitive, directive, and focused. They are the most decisive and assertive out of all styles. They are comfortable with conflict and taking risks. They are individualists who value performance and competence. They struggle the most with being overly direct and not considerate enough.

Guardian

Guardians are reserved, cautious, and methodical. They are detail-oriented, logical, and focused on correcting mistakes. Their decisions are careful and deliberate. They are the most likely out of all styles to incorporate tried-and-true methods. They struggle the most with uncertainty and unstable environments.

Integrator

Integrators are diplomatic, people-oriented, and helpful. For them, consensus and avoiding conflict is essential. They make decisions based on how others would feel about it. They are the most patient and empathetic out of all styles. They struggle the most with overly competitive and emotionally intense environments.
