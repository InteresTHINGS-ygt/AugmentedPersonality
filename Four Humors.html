Your temperament blend is determined by your top two temperaments. The first temperament in your blend will be your highest scoring temperament, the second one will be your second highest scoring temperament.

If your highest scoring temperament is way higher than your second highest score, then you may be composed of a single temperament instead of a blend of two.

Temperaments and blend descriptions are listed below.

Choleric Temperament

The pure choleric temperament. Decisive, action-oriented, and somewhat irritable. They are the natural leaders of temperaments.

Choleric - Melancholic Blend

The most hardworking blend. Goal-oriented and ambitious with an organizational focus. This blend is somewhat reactive as well.

Choleric - Sanguine Blend

This is a highly thrill-seeking and assertive blend. They are action-oriented and instinctive. This is the most impulsive out of all blends.

Choleric - Phlegmatic Blend

In this blend, an ambitious nature is balanced by a steady approach. This blend is relatively unemotional with a steadily driven style.

Melancholic Temperament

The pure melancholic temperament. Reserved, thoughtful, and can be pessimistic. They can often be considered as analytical and precise.

Melancholic - Choleric Blend

This is a very detail-oriented and perfectionistic blend. Highly organized and logical. Conflictual relationship with emotions, morally focused.

Melancholic - Sanguine Blend

This blend is one of the most creative ones. With this blend, there's a sense of idealism. Imaginative, yet prone to disappointment.

Melancholic - Phlegmatic Blend

This is a highly introverted and reserved blend. Often shows a serious attitude to life. Deep and intense emotions, yet emotionally contained.

Sanguine Temperament

The pure sanguine temperament. Enthusiastic, cheerful, optimistic. Their happiness is easily visible, yet they can also be disorganized.

Sanguine - Choleric Blend

This is the most extraverted out of all blends. This blend is the life of the party. Highly adventurous and easily bored by routine.

Sanguine - Melancholic Blend

This blend is highly expressive and creative. There's a high level of curiosity with this blend. Often highly changeable emotions.

Sanguine - Phlegmatic Blend

This blend is relaxed and essentially lives in the moment. They are the ones who are always looking at the bright side. Can be somewhat unfocused.

Phlegmatic Temperament

The pure phlegmatic temperament. Relaxed, peaceful, and can be lazy. Can be stubborn and unwilling to change, yet also stable.

Phlegmatic - Choleric Blend

This blend is the ultimate negotiator. They are the natural caretakers of the temperaments. This blend is stable and reliable like a rock.

Phlegmatic - Melancholic Blend

This blend is reflective and takes the slow-and-steady approach to most things. They are empathetic and willing to sacrifice themselves for others.

Phlegmatic - Sanguine Blend

This blend can easily be happy with the small things in life. They are satisfied by themselves. But they can run the risk of overconsumption.
